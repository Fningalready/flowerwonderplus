Daisy
Rebirth, new beginnings, and hope.

White - purity and innocence(Moulton, 2022).

Yellow - cheerfulness, friendship, joy, and wishing someone well or "get well"(Moulton, 2022).

Blue - long-lasting trust, honesty and loyalty(Moulton, 2022).

Purple - feminine beauty, pride, elegance, and royalty or regalness(Moulton, 2022).

Pink - mild appreciation, tenderness, or adoration(Moulton, 2022).

Red - romantic love(Moulton, 2022).

Orange - passion, excitement, sunshine, and happiness(Moulton, 2022).

Edelweiss
The Edelweiss' distinctive hairy petals and leaves hint to its romantic significance, but it's the white hue that represents purity and innocence(Fleming, 2019).
